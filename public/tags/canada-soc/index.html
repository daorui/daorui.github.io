Canada Soil Organic Carbon (SOC) Change in Response to Climate Change

published in ‘in Preparation’, 2025.03 Abstract Canada’s soils, particularly its vast peatlands, play a critical role in the global carbon cycle due to their significant stores of soil organic carbon (SOC). However, the response of SOC stocks to future climate change remains uncertain, especially given the sensitivity of peatland carbon to rising temperatures. Here, we assess potential changes in SOC stocks across Canada under future climate scenarios by integrating CMIP6 climate projections, machine learning models, and a carbon balance framework.
